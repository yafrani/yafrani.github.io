Research interests

  • Optimisation: problems with multiple interdependent components, fitness landscape analysis, modelling, decomposability in large scale problems

  • Machine learning: theoretical machine learning, neural networks, probabilistic models, evolutionary machine learning
  • Application areas: scheduling, renewable energy, supply chain, etc.
