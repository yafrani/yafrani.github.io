Welcome to my website !

My name is Mohamed El Yafrani. I am a PhD student at Mohammed V university in Rabat, Morocco. The focus of my work is on the use of metaheuristics and evolutionary algorithms to solve optimization problems.

Research interest

  • Decomposability of large scale problems
  • Problems with multiple interdependent components
  • Metaheuristics: Simulated Annealing, Local Search, Tabu Search, Variable Neighborhood Search
  • Evolutionary computation: Genetic Algorithms, Memetic Algorithms, Coevolution
