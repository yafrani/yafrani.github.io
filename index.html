Welcome to my website !

My name is Mohamed El Yafrani, I am a PhD student at Mohammed V university in Rabat, Morocco. The focus of my PhD work is on the use of metaheuristics and evolutionary algorithms to solve optimisation problems with mutliple interdependent sub-problems.

Research interest

  • Problems with multiple interdependent components
  • Decomposability in large scale problems
  • Metaheuristics: Simulated Annealing, Tabu Search, Variable Neighborhood Search, Iterated Local Search
  • Evolutionary computation: Genetic Algorithms, Memetic Algorithms, Genetic Programming, Cooperative Coevolution
  • Machine learning: Deep Neural Networks, Feature Learning, Evolutionary Machine Learning

Research experience

  • October 2016: Visiting student, Optimisation and Logistics group, The University of Adelaide, Australia (Supervisor: Dr. Markus Wagner)
  • December 2014: PhD candidate, LRIT, Mohammed V university in Rabat, Morocco (Supervisor: Pr. Belaïd Ahiod)
